We can also think about inclusivity and access in terms of geographic representation. Let’s first take a look at the U.S. Where do U.S. Olympic athletes come from?
The colors on the map show the median household incomes of US counties and each red dot indicates the hometown of a US Olympic athlete. We see that, on the whole, athletes are more likely to come from areas with higher median household incomes.
